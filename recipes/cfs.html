Chicken Fried Steak

Description

A Southern favorite, these crispy, breaded, tenderized cube steaks drenched in creamy gravy can be served for breakfast or dinner. It's by far the best chicken fried steak I've ever had. I've made this numerous times for my picky son and my Southern-raised better half, as well as for other family and friends and each time, I get nothing but rave reviews.

Ingredients

  • 4 (1/2 pound) beef cube steaks
  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce (e.g. Tabasco™)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups vegetable shortening for frying
  • 4 cups milk
  • kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
